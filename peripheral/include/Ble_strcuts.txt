One important consideration for connectable advertising is whether to resume advertising after a
 peripheral disconnects from a central device. Starting from version 3.0.0 of the nRF Connect SDK,
 advertising is automatically turned off when a peripheral connects to a central device,
 and it remains off after the disconnection. To resume advertising after a disconnection, we need
 to capture the .recycled event from the Bluetooth LE host and trigger the a work queue to
restart advertising again.

Note,  not to call bt_le_adv_start()  directly from .recycled.  this advert fucnc call should 
be  treated as a callback handler or ISR . this ISR should be submitted  to workqueue once the 
perpherial is disconneceted from the central .
